About the Author

Marwane Lamtaifi is a Moroccan author whose poetic storytelling and emotional depth have shaped his voice from a young age. He began his writing journey at 17, sharing his earliest reflections and pieces through online blogs, where readers first discovered his sensitivity to language and imagery.

At 19, he published his first short novel, marking the beginning of a fast-growing literary path. Today, Marwane continues to craft novels and poetry that explore love, memory, loss, and the quiet shadows that follow the human heart. His writing blends lyrical prose with cinematic detail, creating emotional landscapes that feel both intimate and universal.

Driven by a desire to give form to the feelings many struggle to express, he writes stories that echo long after the last page, inviting readers to reflect, to feel, and to rediscover themselves through the power of words.
