About This Blog

How to? is a weekly blog about writing, It is a space where I share how writing truly works, not the myths, not the shortcuts, but the practice behind the pages. Each week, I explore how to start, how to continue, how to finish, and how to live with writing as a daily discipline. This blog is built from experience, blank pages, rejected drafts, published books, and the quiet persistence it takes to keep going. You will find practical advice, clear methods, and honest reflections from a writer still learning, still questioning, still writing.
How to? is not here to teach you how to become famous. It is here to teach you how to become consistent. How to think like a writer. How to stay when doubt appears. How to turn silence into sentences. Because writing begins the same way every time with a question. How to?
